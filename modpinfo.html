Text Message Alerts

To enable text message alerts:
  • Click the Terms and Conditions/Privacy Policy link, to read important terms and conditions.
  • If you agree, click the Opt In checkbox.
  • Enter your mobile phone number.
  • Click Submit.

To complete your opt-in, wait for a message on your mobile phone, then follow the directions in the confirmation message.
