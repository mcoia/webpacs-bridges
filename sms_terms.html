Company Overview

SMS Alerts Service Description

The SMS Alerts service gives you the option to receive text messages from on your mobile device. After you opt into SMS Alerts, will send you text messages to bring an event to your attention, such as an overdue item or a hold ready for pickup. Contact the library for specific information about when you'll receive alerts.

You can choose to opt out of the SMS Alerts service at any time.

Pricing: Standard message charges apply.

Short Code Information

All Alert services are originated from short codes 82453 or 35143. You will never receive any content from from any other source.

Service/Help info

To get help with the SMS Alert text message service, you may email us at .

You can also request our contact information from your mobile phone at any time by sending a text message with the word HELP to 82453 or 35143

Opt In/Opt-Out info

To opt out from the Alerts, send a text message with the word STOP, STOP ALL, END, QUIT, CANCEL or UNSUBSCRIBE to 82453 or 35143 from your mobile phone and we will unsubscribe you from our service. You will not receive any additional text messages via your mobile phone.

You may also opt out by logging into your patron record and updating your patron record.

Frequency of program service

Alert is a monthly subscription service. will send SMS alerts whenever you have new items that have become available for you to pick up. The number of messages received is based on account settings. Although does not charge for this service, standard message charges apply.
